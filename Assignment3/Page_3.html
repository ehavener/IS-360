How it works

Features

Request a ride on dontdrive.life or call 775-000-0000 to get picked up. The nearest team of designated drivers receives the ride request and proceeds to pick the customer up. Once the DD has greeted the customer and identified necessary information on vehicle registration and insurance, the DD will safely drive the customer home in their own car. The other driver follows in another car to transport the DD once the ride has been completed.

Payment Options

When the customer arrives at their destination they can pay via Tile (Credit/Debit) or cash.

Community

Mission

Don’t Drive’s mission, to reduce the prevalence of drunk driving, would not be made possible without our passionate drivers. Don’tDrive is all about bringing the community together to generate a solution for drinking and driving. Each driver is a member of the local community, and has made the Don’t Drive pledge. The Don’t Drive pledge is a company wide pledge to never endanger someone on the roads. “I pledge to never drink and drive. I pledge I will not let someone drink and drive”. Don’t Drive believes it isn’t enough for one person to pledge to never drink and drive. For this tragic issue to be solved, our community must make the pledge to net let someone drink and drive ever. Don’t Drive believes deeply in the power of a connected community. Every person who takes this pledge and shares their passion for this issue on their social media will get a free ride. The free ride will represent the life that person may have saved by sharing their post. The Don’t Drive Pledge is shared with all friends, family, and community members. Through word of mouth, people in the community will stay connected with a trusted sober driver at all times. Each one our drivers report directly to a local Team Leader that can manage all customer complaints. Everyone is encouraged to be involved, and should contact their local Team Leaders for information on becoming a DD. Through Don’t Drive’s transportation network and a strong passionate community the issue of Drunk Driving will finally solved.
