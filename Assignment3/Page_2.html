Why Don’tDrive?

Our Mission

Don’tDrive is making difference in the community through significantly reducing drinking and driving. The main reason why drunk driving occurs is because of the inconvenience people experience when needing to get their car home after a few drinks.

How?

Don’tDrive solves this problem through being the only transportation network in Reno, Nevada that drives customers home in their own cars. Dont’Drive provides designated drivers to those who wish to enjoy their night out without the stress of getting their car home later. Customers are driven home in the comfort of their own car by a insured, licensed, and responsible DD. Don’tDrive provides an affordable and convenient alternative to driving home intoxicated or dealing with Uber, Lyft, or Taxis. Dont’Drive is helping reduce the number of drunk drivers on our roads while eliminating the chance of car vandalism.
