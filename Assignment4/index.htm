XV is an underground rapper from Wichita, KS who had some hype back in the early 2010s. He eventually signed to Warner Bros after his single 'Awesome' made the charts in 2012. It seemed like he was on the path to blow up, but for some reason he just never did. He released over 10 mixtapes, but never was able to drop his debut album, "The Kid With The Green Backpack", even after 10 years of hype and puchbacks. His unique sound was in part thanks to his friend and producer 'Seven.' They worked together constantly until one day they suddenly never made track together again. Shortly after that, around 2014, XV just disappeared off the map. Nobody had any idea why. That was until last week, just as his last fans had all but forgotten about him, he announced his album was coming. Soon.
